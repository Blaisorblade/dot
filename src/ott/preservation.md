
Preservation
============

Statement
---------
$$ \forall [[G]], [[s]], [[t]], [[T]], [[t']], [[s']] $$

if

$$ [[G |= s]] $$
$$ [[G, s |- t : T]] $$
$$ [[t|s --> t'|s']] $$

then

$$ \exists [[G']], [[T']] $$

such that

$$ [[G]] \subseteq [[G']] $$
$$ [[G' |= s']] $$
$$ [[G', s' |- t' : T']] $$
$$ [[G', s' |- T' <: T]] $$
